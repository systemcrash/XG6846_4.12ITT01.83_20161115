LAN Port Disable/Enable Configuration
If a port is disabled, the PC connected on that port will not get ethernet link.
If a port is Follow WAN Down, the port link up/down will follow WAN link up/down.

Follow WAN Down Time (sec)
LAN1 : Disable   Enable   Follow WAN Down
LAN2 : Disable   Enable   Follow WAN Down
LAN3 : Disable   Enable   Follow WAN Down
LAN4 : Disable   Enable   Follow WAN Down
LAN5 : Disable   Enable
